🔍 POS Button Verification Tool

Maelekezo:
1. Bofya button ya "Open POS" hapo chini
2. Angalia kama unaona "SPLIT PAYMENT" button
3. Kama hauioni, rudi hapa na bofya "Force Clear Cache"

Expected Buttons:

  • ✓ Discount button (%) - Karibu na trash icon
  • ✓ SPLIT PAYMENT button - Chini ya PROCESS PAYMENT

Cash Balance Calculation:

Formula:
Cash Balance = Starting Cash + Cash In - Cash Out + Cash Sales - Cash Refunds

Data Source:
• Starting Cash: Kutoka kwenye shift uliyoanza
• Transactions: Kutoka kwenye cash_register_transactions table
